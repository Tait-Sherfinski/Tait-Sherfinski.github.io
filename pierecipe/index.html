The Pie

The recipe itself is relatively simple and can be made by almost anyone. It is thickened with flour, cornstarch, and egg yolks and contains no milk. It takes around 40 minutes to make and consists of 8 servings of the pie.

Ingredients Needed

All ingredients are listed below:

  • White Sugar
  • All Puropose Flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Lemon(s)
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Pie Crust

Equipment Needed

The only thing needed for this recipe is an oven which should be preheated to 350F or 175C
